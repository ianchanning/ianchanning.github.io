The BCD Test

“Take this once a month and come back and see me when the voices stop being so loud...”

  1. Pick five words to describe yourself.
  2. What is your greatest fear?
  3. What's your most unpleasant characteristic?
  4. When did you last cry, and why?
  5. What turns you on?
  6. Happiness is...
  7. What characteristics have you inherited from your parents?
  8. What do you live for?
  9. What were you like when you were at school?
  10. What are you trying to change in your life?
  11. What would drive you to commit suicide?
  12. What is your most treasured possession?
  13. Recite a line of poetry.
  14. What music would you have at your funeral?
  15. Name a book or film that has affected your life in some way and how?
  16. What kind of people do you hate?
  17. Name someone you are jealous of, and why?
